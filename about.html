Our Heritage

Pioneering Vertical Mobility in Kerala

Incorporated in 2018, Kalyan Elevators Private Limited has established itself as an engineering pioneer in the vertical transportation industry. Headquartered in Kochi with our regional division in Thrissur, we design, engineer, and maintain luxury residential lifts and commercial elevator systems across Kerala and South India.

Our journey is built on a simple philosophy: vertical transit should be silent, smooth, and in perfect harmony with a building's architecture. Rather than offering one-size-fits-all products, we collaborate closely with leading architects and homeowners to build customized cabins, shafts, and control interfaces that match the design intent of each individual space.
