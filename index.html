About Us

Kalyan Elevators — India's Premier Bespoke Elevator Brand

Founded in Kochi, Kerala, Kalyan Elevators has established itself as an engineering pioneer in the vertical transportation industry. Specializing in high-end, bespoke home elevators, panoramic capsule lifts, and commercial transit systems, we blend advanced technology with sophisticated European engineering standards like EN 81-41 and TÜV SÜD certification.

Our systems are designed for luxury residences and commercial landmarks across South India, offering whisper-quiet traction drives, space-efficient hydraulic systems, and robust Automatic Rescue Devices (ARD). From zero-pit residential lifts to smart, multi-car dispatch systems, every Kalyan installation is a commitment to safety, architectural harmony, and silent elegance.

Luxury Home Lifts

Crafting the center of
your private estate.

Kalyan Elevators redefines luxury living with home lifts designed in quiet dialogue with modern architecture. Tailored specifically for villas, bungalows, and premium penthouses, our home elevator range integrates seamlessly into existing structures without requiring major civil alterations or massive construction shafts.

Using space-saving **Machine Room-Less (MRL)** gearless traction and quiet hydraulic drives, we achieve whisper-quiet travel with zero pit and zero headroom requirements. Compliant with the stringent European safety code **EN 81-41**, every residential installation features built-in Automatic Rescue Devices (ARD) that guarantee safety for your family during power variations.

From custom wood veneers and gold-anodized mirror frames to panoramic glass shafts that read like sculptures, every Kalyan home lift is a unique creation, engineered once, for one specific address.
